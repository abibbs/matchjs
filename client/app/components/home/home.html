MatchJS

Get Matched with a JavaScript Mentor or Mentee today!


Connect and Meet With Local JavaScript Developers

MatchJS is an app designed to connect current and aspiring software engineers seeking to improve their skills in web development with mentors who have real-world experience and are willing to share their knowledge.

Ignite Your Learning

With JavaScript frameworks constantly falling in and out of favor it can be difficult to gain a foothold in the ever-changing landscape. By allowing mentees to follow would be mentors and connect via shared learning goals, MatchJS is aiming to make the way we learn web development a more open and communal experience.
